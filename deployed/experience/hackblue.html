HackBlue

HackBlue is a student organization I started in the Fall of 2017 with with a mission to spread a love for computer science and technology to underrepreesnted communities in the local area. We create lasting relationships with local after-school programs to introduce students to the possibilites of programming to create pathways for education and professional success in technology.

What we do

HackBlue gives students from underserved communities exposure to technology in three main ways: Weekly Mentorship, Development Workshops, and Keynote Speakers.

Weekly Mentorship

Our bread and butter; by meeting with students once or twice a week to teach code, we give them the opportunities for success in technology

Development Workshop

We offer one-off development workshops to introduce students to the basics of computer science to learn the possibilities of code

Keynote Speakers

In order to expose students to the cutting edge technologies, we offer keynote speakers with leaders in the field and trips to local startups
